Drinks

Immerse yourself in our indulgent drinks menu, where every selection is crafted to delight and inspire. Begin with our luxurious lattes, featuring rich espresso blended with velvety steamed milk and infused with decadent flavors like caramel, hazelnut, and vanilla bean. If you're in the mood for something refreshing, try our handcrafted iced coffees, available in flavors such as creamy vanilla and mocha mint, or enjoy a frosty frappé, bursting with blended flavors and a drizzle of syrup. Our specialty hot chocolate is a must-try, made with real chocolate and topped with marshmallows or whipped cream. Each drink is designed to provide a moment of indulgence, making your visit a truly delightful experience.

At Sleepy Hog Cafe, our matcha is a vibrant ritual that transforms your day. Crafted from premium, ceremonial-grade matcha, each cup begins with hand-picked leaves, carefully ground into a fine powder that boasts a stunning emerald hue. As our skilled baristas whisk the matcha to a frothy perfection, the rich, earthy aroma fills the air, inviting you to indulge. The first sip reveals a harmonious blend of creamy sweetness and subtle umami, with a smooth texture that glides across your palate. Choose it served hot for a comforting embrace or iced for a refreshing twist, perhaps enhanced with a splash of almond milk or a drizzle of honey. Each matcha is not just a drink but a moment of mindfulness, perfect for recharging your spirit amidst the cozy ambiance of our cafe. Experience the artistry and tranquility of our matcha at Sleepy Hog Cafe, where every cup is a celebration of nature’s finest flavors.
